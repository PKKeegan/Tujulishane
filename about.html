Welcome To Tujulishane

Tujulishane means ‘Let’s Educate Each Other’. We believe in the power of empowering individuals and communities to be agents of change. It is around this ideology that we formulate and implement multi-faceted projects across a diverse range of communities, working to create meaningful impact among the vulnerable and marginized, with a focus on adolescents, women and children. Tujulishane is built on these values:

Partner with us

Mission

Our mission is to make development and humanitarian aid better. Our top priority is to ensure that our work really helps those in need, in the best way. Anything else is secondary.

Vision

An empowered and gender-equal society in which all people and communities are safe, healthy, and thriving.

Strategic Approach

This mindset aligns our thinking with that of the individual and communities. All our decisions are made with the efficiency and success of our projects in mind.

Tu-Engage!!

Integrity

We take responsibility, are unyieldingly honest, say what we mean and match our behaviour to our words. Always!

Sustainability

We prioritize sustainability in our programs and initiatives, considering the long-term impact on people, the planet, and future generations. We seek solutions that are environmentally responsible, economically viable, and socially equitable.

Inclusion

We are committed to equality, justice, and leaving no one behind.

Tujulishane delivers critical transformational programmes on children, adolescents, youth, women and men and has a multi-pronged approach that has guaranteed success at every level of engagement in implemented programmes.

Learn More

Tranformation

We drive change through innovation and empowerment, fostering lasting impact in communities. Our actions align with our vision, inspiring positive change and resilience. Always evolving for a brighter future.

Transparency

Wherever we can, we publish what we do together with our results, both good and bad.

Community

We value and celebrate the diversity of communities we serve. We believe in the strength of community connections and the importance of fostering belonging, solidarity, and shared purpose.

Adolescents and Young People Sexual and Reproductive Health

Empower young people with comprehensive education, accessible services, and meaningful engagement, ensuring their rights to health, autonomy, and well-being are realized.

Sexual and Gender Based Violence

Combating SGBV with comprehensive legal frameworks, survivor-centered services, community prevention, and efforts to dismantle harmful gender norms, fostering healing and justice for survivors.

Child and Maternal Health

Ensure access to quality healthcare for mothers and children worldwide through prenatal care, immunization, nutrition, and community support, fostering preparedness and opportunity for every birth.

Advocacy and Public Education

Drive impactful change through advocacy and education, mobilizing support and influencing policy for critical social issues, fostering a more equitable and informed society.

Sustainable Livelihoods

Foster resilience, equity, and environmental stewardship in communities through income diversification, equitable resource access, climate resilience, and gender empowerment, promoting shared prosperity.

Climate Action

Inspire emission reduction, climate adaptation, and sustainable development. Engage youth, ensure corporate accountability, foster global cooperation, and advocate for justice-centered solutions. Raise awareness of climate interconnectedness, empowering collective action for a sustainable future.
